About Me

PhD candidate of 2020 jointly cultivated by Shanghai Advanced Research Institution, CAS and ShanghaiTech University, coming from Weihai, Shandong Province. I am conducting research related to electrocatalysis/electrosynthesis at the Center for Green Chemical Engineering Technology Research and Development at Shanghai Advanced Research Institution, under the supervision of Professor Hui Yang. Outside of work, I devote a great deal of passion to activities such as playing the piano, symphonic music, vocal performance, badminton, swimming and traveling. üí´

I have published 7 research papers in CAS-Q1-TOP journals such as Angewandte Chemie and Adv. Funct. Mater., notably my first-author paper published on Angewandte Chemie has garnered over 60 citations within a year. Diverse PEM electrolyzers for industrial-scale high-purity hydrogen peroxide electrosynthesis have been designed in my research, successfully demonstrating the feasibility and commercial potential of high-concentration production. As a core research member, I contributed to projects funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) and the Shanghai Natural Science Foundation, achieving significant milestones as the second contributor. Additionally, as a CPC member, I served as a part-time graduate counselor.
